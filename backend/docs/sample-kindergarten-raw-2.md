## Kindergarten A – November 2024 (Monthly Summary)

**School:** Euro Kids International, Berlin Campus  
**Class:** Kindergarten A (Ages 4–6)  
**Teacher:** Ms. Anna Schmidt  
**Period:** November 1–30, 2024  

---

### 1. Student Behavior & Emotional Development

November was a high-energy month for the class, especially during group and competitive activities. Students showed enthusiasm but experienced challenges managing emotions during conflicts and transitions. While they could identify emotions and classroom rules with guidance, many found it difficult to apply these strategies independently in the moment.

---

### 2. Events, Safety & Parent Feedback

The inter-school sports day on November 14 was a key highlight, with strong participation from students. Some conflicts and minor injuries occurred, all of which were managed appropriately with first aid and parent communication. Parents appreciated the engagement but shared concerns about emotional reactions during competitive situations.

---

**End Note:**  
November highlighted strong student engagement alongside the need for continued support in emotional regulation and cooperative behavior.
